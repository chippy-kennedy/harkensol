Build a Project Team

HarKenSol's core team is a gorup of computer science undergrads. For any given project we might bring more coders, designers, management specialists, business majors, doctors, lawyers, doctors. As college students we have access to virtualy any specialty to supplement our programming knowledge. And there is no shortage of eager young entrepenuers to join us and put their best work into your project

Stay Organized

Every HarKenSol project, whether an internal side project or a major contract, is assigned a project manager in charge of the HarKenSol team. This assures that deadlines are met, communication is happening throughout, and as the idea changes, the team is able to change with it

Some Hardcore Coding

Why use undergrads? Because we all live with each other, are way too good at getting homework done, and don't really seem to need sleep. These days anything from a simple webapp to machine integration calls for multiple coding laguages, advanced computer science concepts, and a whole lot of learning on a development team's part. Are promise is to get everything you need done well and on time, even if it takes a couple of all nighters along the way
