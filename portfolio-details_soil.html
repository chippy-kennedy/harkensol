Smart Oil

After being asked by a small-time oil producer to streamline the process of testing and adjusting oil well motors, the HarKenSol team made their own solution. Using Raspberry Pis and Arduinos as the core processing and communication units, many technologies were integrated into the system. As the HarKenSol SmartOil team learns more about oil well production, current and future testing involves integrating Web-App control to the producer, homemade-circuit-based local microcontroller systems, better oil well sensor intergration, and more.
